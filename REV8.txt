Define or Identify: 
The Dust Bowl - Period of dust storms in the west US
Dust pneumonia - Sickness of the lungs from dust being inhaled
Drought - Severe lack of water for a period of time 

1. What were some of the causes of the dust bowl? Drought, Famine, Unemployment, no farms.
2. How long did the drought last?  1931-1938 (7 YEARS)
3. Give at least two examples of dust bowl plagues. Dust Pneumonia and Jack Rabbits
4. Which 5 states were affected by the dust bowl?  Texas, Oklahoma, Kansas, Colorado and New Mexico
